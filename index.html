Contagion
(Mode of Transmission)

Through Air

The virus can spread from an infected person's mouth or nose in small liquid particles when they cough, sneeze, speak, sing or breathe heavily.

Hands Contact

People may become infected by touching these contaminated surfaces, then touching their eyes, noses or mouths without having cleaned their hands first.

Touching objects

The disease can spread from person to person through small droplets from the nose or mouth. These droplets land on objects and surfaces around the person.

Symptoms of Covid-19


Common Symptoms
  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Tiredness
People with mild symptoms who are otherwise healthy should manage their symptoms at home.
Read more on WHO
Less Common Symptoms
  • Aches and Pains
  • Sore Throat & Headache
  • Diarrhoea
  • Conjuctivitis
  • Loss of Taste or Smell
Read more on WHO
Serious Symptoms
  • Difficulty Breathing or Shortness of Breath
  • Chest pain or Pressure
  • Loss of Speech or Movement
Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms.
Read more on WHO

Prevention Protocols

  • Wear A Mask,Save Lives!

    Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.

  • Wash Your Hands Frequently

    Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

  • Social Distancing

    Maintain a Minimum Distance of 6-feet. Say Namaste🙏

  • Avoid Touching Face

    Cover your Nose and Mouth with bent Elbow while Coughing or Sneezing.

  • Stay At Home

    Stay Home if you feel Unwell.

  • Visit Your Doctor

    If you have Fever, Cough and difficulty Breathing, seek medical attention
